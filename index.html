Additional Information

Students must be available during the entire week of CodeLA and from 8am to 8pm on Saturday, April 8th in order to participate. High school credits in technology are available to students who complete the entire Codechella experience. Since students will be missing their regular classes during the week of Codechella, they may need to make up work outside of regular school hours if necessary. TAP cards will be provided for students taking public transportation to LATTC.
